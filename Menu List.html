Kebab

A kebab is a dish made of pieces of meat, fish, or vegetables that are marinated, skewered, and grilled or roasted. Originating in the Middle East, kebabs have become a popular part of Indian cuisine, especially in Mughlai and Awadhi cooking.

Gajar Ka Halwa

Gajar ka Halwa, also known as carrot halwa, is a traditional Indian dessert made from grated carrots, milk, sugar, and ghee. It is slow-cooked to a soft, rich consistency and often flavored with cardamom. The dish is typically garnished with dry fruits like almonds, cashews, and pistachios. It is especially popular during winter and festive occasions like Diwali and Holi. Gajar ka Halwa is loved for its warm, sweet taste and comforting texture.
